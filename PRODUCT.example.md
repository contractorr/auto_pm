# Product Vision

Build a calm, high-signal AI product that helps users stay focused, informed, and steadily improve.

## Target Users

- solo builders
- ambitious generalists
- knowledge workers who want a private system of record

## Non-Goals

- broad social networking
- ad-supported engagement loops
- enterprise workflow customization in the first phase

## Current Strategic Priority

Improve activation and week-one retention for new users by reducing friction in onboarding, learning, and early value discovery.
